The Great Submission Script: How Religion, School, & Society Built Slaves

The Binding Illusion of Religion

Have you ever questioned why, with the world flooded with churches, gurus, and spiritual leaders, society continues to plummet into chaos? Why is morality decaying and order crumbling, despite all the promises of salvation and enlightenment? Could it be that religion plays a far darker role than we’ve been led to believe? Or is there something deeper—more insidious—lurking beneath the surface?

Let’s begin with the word “religion.” It comes from the Latin verb religare, meaning “to bind fast.” And bind, we most certainly have been. The chains are tight—crafted from dogma, systems, rituals, and meaningless ceremonies. This is not true spirituality. True spirituality is the direct experience of The All—a divine force, an understanding of our connection to something vast, omnipotent, and unnameable. It’s an intuitive realization of our sacred relationship with the universe.

But then there’s theology—a man-made construction designed to control and distort this divine connection. These self-proclaimed "middlemen" claim to mediate between us and the divine, but they are nothing more than thieves—stealing our sovereignty, manipulating us with their false promises, and draining our energy for their gain.

The Hijacking of Truth and Progress

The Religion of Truth doesn’t operate in the realm of control and obedience—it is the power to awaken our true selves and reclaim the liberty we were born with. Forget the idea of subjective truths and moral relativism. The truth is objective, it is real, and it is out there waiting for us to rise to it.

Now, let’s talk about the real consequences of this manipulation. In its most distorted form, religion has become a weapon—a powerful tool for stifling progress and warping the human spirit. For scientists, it has been a formidable barrier, eroding reason, suppressing innovation, and holding back discoveries that could have catapulted humanity into a new era. Think of how much further we could have gone if centuries hadn’t been wasted under the chains of dogmatic oppression.

But I’m not here to champion blind worship of science, either. This isn’t about replacing one form of servitude with another. It's about understanding that both religion and science have been hijacked and used to control us. Each in its own way.

Reclaiming Sovereignty and Destroying the Matrix

For those who are truly spiritual, religion can be an even greater threat. Instead of elevating the soul, it often traps the consciousness in a perpetual cycle of guilt, fear, and submission. How is that spiritual? This is not what true freedom or enlightenment looks like. This is a systematic dismantling of our innate power. Far from teaching us morality, many religions have become breeding grounds for immorality: wars fought in the name of invisible gods, hatred justified by ancient texts, and faith used as a weapon to divide and conquer. And yet, so many will refuse to see it—blindly holding onto their belief systems, unwilling to challenge the very prison they’ve locked themselves in.

Let’s not sugarcoat it: history is drenched in blood—blood spilled in the name of religion. The Crusades, the Jihads, the Inquisitions. Wars fought, people slaughtered—all in service of gods who remain silent. These are not the religions of life, love, or truth. They are the religions of death. Systems that pit brother against brother, nation against nation, human against human—all in the name of a higher power who does nothing but watch from the shadows.

Religion, school, society—they have one goal: to break your spirit, to make you believe that you are small, insignificant, and subject to a greater force. That is the matrix. It is an illusion, a system designed to keep you submissive, docile, and chained to the status quo.

But here’s the truth, the raw, unfiltered truth: You are not small. You are not a slave. You are a sovereign being, powerful beyond measure. Your spiritual power, your ability to manifest wealth, your connection to the universe—it is all yours to reclaim.

The matrix is designed to make you think that spirituality is something you need to seek outside yourself. It’s designed to make you think that money is something that only others control. But the truth is, the system is rigged. And it’s time for you to break free.

Forget the religion that binds you to a false god

Forget the schools that teach you to obey and conform. Forget the societal structures that have kept you trapped in mediocrity. It’s time to rise, reclaim your sovereignty, and embrace the life that is yours to create.

This is not just a spiritual awakening. It’s a total transformation—a rebellion against the forces that have tried to keep you small. You are a part of the divine, and it’s time you remembered that. It’s time you realized the power you’ve always had. Wake up. The matrix is not your reality. The truth is.

Now, it’s up to you. Will you continue to live in the shadows, bound by systems that were never designed for your greatness? Or will you rise and reclaim your true power? The choice is yours.

Remember: true spirituality doesn’t demand your submission—it awakens your sovereignty. And once you’ve awakened, nothing can stop you.
